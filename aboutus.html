About Us

Davanagere Bombat Mirchi Masala is a chat shop located in Basaveshwar Nagar, Bangalore. Basavaraj J C, a native of Davanagere started this shop in the year 2012. This is the shop where you can taste famous Davanagere food items. The food items are made with Davanagere's traditional making style without any modifications. The people working here are also from Davanagere and all the items available in this shop are prepared by them only. We are glad to give you the taste of Davanagere.
